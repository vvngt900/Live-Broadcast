Routes Broadcast

Routes is introducing live broadcasting for taxi passengers creating an immersive travel experience. The best part for passengers lies in real-time engagement during their journey. Users can tune into live broadcasts showcasing local insights, events, or even interactive sessions. This feature transforms commuting into a dynamic, informative, and entertaining adventure. Passengers gain access to destination highlights, cultural snippets, or travel tips shared by hosts. It fosters connectivity, making each ride more than just a commute, turning it into an opportunity for discovery and connection with the surroundings.
